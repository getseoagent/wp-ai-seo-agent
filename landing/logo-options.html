Quick take

  • A — most product-specific (tells the whole sample-and-extrapolate story in 32px). Best for wp.org icon where reviewers want to instantly grok what the plugin does.
  • B — most universally readable. "Approve in chat" is clear in any context. Slight risk: lots of chat apps use similar marks.
  • C — most distinctive / contrarian. Will read as "transformation" but requires a second to interpret. Memorable once you know what it is.
  • D — pure brandmark. Works as a long-term brand asset; doesn't try to explain the product. Pairs best with strong wordmark.

My pick: A if you want the logo to do storytelling work, D if you want a clean brand letter that scales to T-shirt prints later. B and C are solid backups, not first picks.
